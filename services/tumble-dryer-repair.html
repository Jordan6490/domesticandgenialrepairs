Tumble dryer repair

Dryers are a valuable, time-saving appliance. Most people are pretty obsessed with this modern laundry-room convenience, thus it may be terribly frustrating if issues occur. Even if your dryer is not fully broken, it may be uneconomical to keep running if it is not operating with efficiency. If you have distinguished spikes in your energy bills, slow drying, or another…

Gas dryer Repair or Vent Maintenance

Dryers have a rotating drum known as a tumbler that circulates heated air and evaporates moisture. The humid air created from this method is pushed through the emission system to the surface of your house, creating room for dry air to enhance the drying process. Reliable Appliance Repairs technicians will repair any part of this system or clean your dryer vent to keep your home safe and running smoothly.

There are 2 main styles of dryers that we repair:

• Electric - These dryers cost a bit but gas ones up-front and also are easier and less expensive to put in. Exhaust requirements are terribly minimal: steam emission only.

• Gas - Dryers operating on natural gas or propane cost a touch additional up-front than electrical dryers, however, they end up being the more economical choice over time. Specialized venting is needed.

With their specialized knowledge of your kind of drier, our team of Gas Safe registered engineers can offer a fast diagnosing and viable answer for the matter.
